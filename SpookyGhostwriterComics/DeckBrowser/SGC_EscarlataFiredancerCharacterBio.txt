Art isn't the only way Cassandra knows how to express herself! Dance is one of Cassandra's main hobbies, and for a brief time, she incorporated it into her superhero persona. Escarlata's Firedancer phase combined the graceful movements of flamenco dancing with the destructive force of napalm.

The other New Granwall Guardians were confused by this development, but were supportive. However, within a week, Escarlata realized that a dress was an inconvenient outfit to fight in and went back to her normal attire, saying "let's never speak of this again". Electrogeist still teases her about this occasionally.
